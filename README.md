Pizza Sales Performance Dashboard | Power BI Project

Overview

This project focuses on analyzing pizza sales data to uncover business trends, customer purchasing behavior, product performance, and revenue drivers.

Using Power BI, SQL, and Excel, I transformed raw sales transactions into an interactive dashboard that helps stakeholders monitor key business metrics and make data-driven decisions.

The dashboard provides a complete view of sales performance, highlighting what is selling well, what is underperforming, and where the business can improve profitability.

Key Business Insights
Revenue Leader

The Thai Chicken Pizza generated the highest revenue, indicating strong customer demand for premium menu options.

Most Popular Pizza

The Classic Deluxe Pizza led in both order count and quantity sold, making it the most frequently purchased product.

Customer Preference

Large pizzas contributed nearly 46% of total revenue, showing a strong preference for larger order sizes.

Peak Demand Day

Thursday recorded the highest number of orders, outperforming even Friday and Saturday.

Product Optimization Opportunity

The Brie Carre Pizza consistently ranked lowest in revenue, quantity sold, and order volume, suggesting a potential menu optimization opportunity.

Seasonal Trend

Revenue peaked during July and experienced noticeable declines during September and October.
